Welcome to Heartjoy Studio!

Jo has always explored the creative side of her nature through beading, papier Mache, paper crafts, drawing, scrapbooking and creating theme based decorations for numerous events.

In 2014 Jo’s love of the arts expanded as she began experimenting with mixed media to create pieces of artwork. Jo utilises a variety of methods to create her pieces. Her artwork features acrylic paints, inks, paper and water based pigment sticks in the creation of whimsical pieces.

Through her painting, Jo finds inner peace and happiness which is reflected in the development of her unique style and distinctive use of colour. Over the last 5 years Jo’s talent has been recognised by her growing circle of admirers of her work, resulting in an award and numerous commissions for unique pieces.

Her Facebook and Instagram page “Heartjoy Studio” is gaining in momentum as her pieces of art travel Australia wide. Her ability to create life-like portraits has seen her receive several commissions to capture moments in time.
